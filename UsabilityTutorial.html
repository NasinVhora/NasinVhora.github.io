KRUG’S FIRST LAW OF USABILITY

Usability means users can easily access and understand the controls of webpages without any confusion.
For e.g: For Submit or Save button, we can use green color. For Cancel button use "red" color.

Scanning, Satisficing, And Muddling Through

Scanning = Users are not intereseted to read all the contents of the webpage.Focus on the main contents rather than other things.
Satisficing, And Muddling = Satisfy the user by fullfill the requirement of webpage.

Designing For Scanning, Not Reading

Make webpages effectively and efficiently.
User wants to read important informations only.
Focus on the main point.

Why users like mindless choices

Create that webpage which has a specific meaning of dropdown and button.
User doesn't like to read all the contents or click many times.
Be specific for your actions.

The art of not writing for the web

Write easy and informative contents of the webpage, so that users can easily understand what we try to explain.
Your contents must have useful information.

Street signs and Breadcrumbs: Designing navigation

Use navigation in your webpage.
By using navigation, we can easily move forward or backword to the webpages.
Use "Link" if you directly want to move the another page.
